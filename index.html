How to log labor

  1. 1Open the monthly PM work order.
  2. 2Go to Labor Tasks and add a new line.
  3. 3In Description, enter the date first.
  4. 4Select your name, enter hours spent, then save and exit.
DO NOT CLOSE THE WORK ORDER — leave it open for the next technician.
